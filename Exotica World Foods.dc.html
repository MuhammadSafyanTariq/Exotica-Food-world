Our Story

Serving Swansea, one family at a time

For years, Exotica World Foods has been serving Swansea with quality groceries and authentic foods from around the world. We believe every family deserves access to fresh ingredients and international flavours — all under one roof.

THE START
Opened our doors on St Helen's Road with a simple promise: fresh food, fair prices.
GROWING
